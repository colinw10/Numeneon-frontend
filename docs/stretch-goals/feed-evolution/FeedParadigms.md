## Where Everyone Is Stuck

All current feeds optimize for **one** of these:

```
TIME        â†’ "What's newest?" (chronological)
POPULARITY  â†’ "What's trending?" (engagement)
RELEVANCE   â†’ "What's for me?" (algorithmic)
```

**The problem:** These are all **consumption-centric**. They answer "what should I see?" but not:

- "What do I _need_ to see?"
- "What's _missing_ from my perspective?"
- "What would _challenge_ me?"
